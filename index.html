My Digital Flipbooks

My work explores the intersection of art, mythology, and travel, aiming to bring ancient narratives into a modern context.

Mythology: I delve into global mythologies, folklore, and epic tales, studying how these stories shape cultural identity and our understanding of the world.

Travel: My travels serve as vital field research, tracking the physical and cultural landscapes that birthed these myths, from the temples of Greece to the ancient sites of the Maya.

Art: The core of my projects is the creative interpretation of these themes, resulting in visual art, written pieces, and multimedia presentations that re-imagine and celebrate the enduring power of these stories and places.
