Welcome to RKG Projects

My current projects explore the dynamic synergy between Personal Travel, ancient Mythology, and cutting-edge AI Digital Art. I often embark on journeys to sites of historical and mythological significance such as the forests of the Norse sagas or the pilgrimage routes of Japanese Shinto (Personal Travel). At these locations, I collect atmospheric data, natural imagery, and write down the resonant myths and local folklore.

My Digital Flipbooks

My ongoing project involves creating a series of digital flipbooks that capture the ephemeral beauty of my personal travel experiences, rendered in the distinctive, whimsical style of Studio Ghibli. Each flipbook transforms mundane moments a quiet café in Kyoto, a sudden rain shower over the Scottish Highlands, or a train ride through the Italian countryside into short, animated sequences.
